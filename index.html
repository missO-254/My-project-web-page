Why Lumina?

Many of us carry unseen psychological wounds - from childhood trauma, broken family structures, toxic environments, or painful life events. These wounds shape our beliefs, affect relationships, and often keep us from living consciously and reaching our full potential. Unlike physical injuries, these wounds are invisible but deeply impactful.

Lumina was born from my own personal journey and a deep commitment to create a safe space for healing and growth, helping others to unlock their full potential and rediscover their true path and joy.

As a young girl, I always dreamt of a happy, fulfilling life. I had a childhood filled with joy and good memories, but little did I know that losing my dad at a very tender age would profoundly shape my relationships and sense of worth. It wasn't until adulthood that I began to understand how this loss had affected me. Through a journey of deep inner work and self-reflection, I began to heal, and that journey inspired me to create Lumina. I share this to remind you: whatever you've been through, healing and purpose are always possible.
